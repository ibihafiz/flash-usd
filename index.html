How to Use:

  1. Install TronLink wallet extension for your browser
  2. Connect your wallet (make sure you're on Nile Testnet)
  3. Get test TRX from Nile Testnet faucet for transaction fees
  4. Enter the amount of USDT you want to mint
  5. Set expiration time in seconds (3600 = 1 hour)
  6. Click "Mint Flash USDT" and confirm the transaction

Note: The minted tokens will automatically appear in your TronLink wallet after successful transaction.
